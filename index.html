    The virtual platform

    It is a virtual platform created by students of grades 10° and 11° of the educational institution “Sol de oriente”, with the tutoring of English teachers and systems of the same institution. The main objective of this platform is to facilitate the work of the English teacher using the technological resources. At the same time, motivate students to learn English in an interactive and original way.
